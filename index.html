Clues, Reveal & Notepad Policy

Clue Decks are tiered: 3 / 4 / 6 / 8 clues (Flirt / High Octane / Insider / Cloud 9). ▼

Each ticket tier includes a specific number of clues in your personal Clue Deck. Higher tiers receive more clues, giving you additional information about potential matches and increasing your chances of finding compatible connections during the event.

Live reveal is included for all guests; higher tiers receive priority in the reveal queue. ▼

Every guest will experience the live reveal during the event, where matches are announced. Higher tier ticket holders receive priority placement in the reveal queue, meaning they will be matched earlier in the evening.

Every guest receives a Spark Notes Notepad at check-in. List any badge # or name you also liked. ▼

Upon arrival, each guest receives a Spark Notes Notepad to jot down information about people you'd like to connect with further. Simply note their badge number or name to indicate interest.

By turning in your notepad, you consent to Club Matched contacting those guests after the event to request their permission for an intro. We never share contact details without mutual consent. ▼

When you submit your Spark Notes Notepad at the end of the evening, you authorize Club Matched to reach out to the individuals you've noted. We will ask for their permission before sharing any contact information. We respect privacy and only facilitate introductions when both parties have expressed mutual interest.
